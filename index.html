// 01 — About

Asia's Largest Sci-Tech Festival

Techfest, the annual Science & Technology festival of IIT Bombay, was founded in 1998. Under the patronage of UNESCO, UNICEF, Make in India, and Digital India, it has become Asia's largest Sci-Tech festival — drawing students, researchers, innovators, and global speakers every December to the IIT Bombay campus.

// 02 — Competitions

Flagship Competitions

Battle it out in India's most prestigious technical competitions. Every event is open to all college students across India and internationally.

🤖
International Robowars

Design and build combat robots that battle in a bulletproof arena. Teams from across the world compete in one of Techfest's most iconic events. Weight categories apply.

Prize: ₹ Disclosed at fest Robotics
🧠
HackAI — Hackathon

A flagship AI-focused hackathon where teams build innovative solutions across coding, machine learning, and cybersecurity domains over 24–48 hours.

Prize: ₹ Disclosed at fest AI / Hackathon
📈
Algo Trading Challenge

Design algorithms that trade on simulated markets and compete on live leaderboards. The 2025 edition featured a prize pool of ₹5,00,000.

Prize: ₹5,00,000 Fintech
🚁
International Drone Racing

High-speed international drone racing through obstacle courses, plus spectacular drone light shows on the IIT Bombay campus.

Prize: ₹ Disclosed at fest Drones
🎮
Gamers League

Esports competition at a national scale under the Techfest umbrella. The 2025 edition had a total prize pool of ₹4,00,000 across multiple titles.

Prize: ₹4,00,000 Esports
🛡️
Capture The Flag (CTF)

Jeopardy-style cybersecurity competition spanning web exploitation, forensics, cryptography, and reverse engineering — with escalating difficulty levels.

Prize: ₹ Disclosed at fest Cybersecurity
🦾
Global Humanoid Conclave

Techfest 2025 featured a humanoid robot performing live on stage. This showcase brings together the cutting edge of robotics, AI, and human-machine interaction.

Showcase Event Humanoid AI
⚡
Cyborg Landing Page Dev

Design and develop a responsive landing page based on a cyborg theme with creative UI/UX and modern design elements. Submit your GitHub link and a short demo recording via Google Drive.

Reward: 50 Coins Web Dev · UI/UX
🛡️
Defence Expo & Symposium

Featuring former Army, Navy, and Air Force chiefs. Live weapon showcases, NSG K9 demonstrations, and cutting-edge defence technology exhibitions.

Open Exhibition Defence
